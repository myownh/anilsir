Shade Sails Manufacturer/Supplier

Economical and Attractive Shade Sails that enhance your Outdoor Space

Modular shade sails will create outdoor spaces that protect from wind, dust, sun, and rain, snow in an economical and aesthetically pleasing way. They are manufactured in such a way that you can easily combine various shade sail shapes to create an architectural outdoor space.

Why Invest in Shade Sails?

Shade sails are not only visually attractive but also effectively protect against the sun & rain without any restrictions. They are a cheaper alternative to awnings and pergolas. The strength of Sky Arc Tensile Structures and Products components and installation enables them to resist the most adverse winds and sun rays.

Available in a variety of Fabrics, colors and no need for maintenance, they combine elegance and good protection against weather.

Types of Shade Sails and Key Features

Designed to meet your needs, our wide range of shade sails would help build a space that suits your tastes and purpose of the installation.

DiffereSky Arcforms from square to triangle are available and can be arranged in a modular way to cover your commercial space or living area. A four sided sail would provide more shade than a three sided sail, but three sided sails are more attractive in appearance. To shade a large area, multiple three sided sails can be arranged side by side or superimposed.

  • Highly quality fabrics (280 gsm) with waterproofing and tear resistance
  • Unique styles and sophisticated designs, adaptable to your space
  • Strong MS steel used for poles and general hardware, with fine powder coating for best-in-class finishing
